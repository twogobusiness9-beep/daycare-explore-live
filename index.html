Our Approach

Freedom within limits, guided by trust and respect

Following Dr. Maria Montessori’s method, we offer children real choices within clear, caring boundaries — nurturing independence, concentration, and a genuine love of learning.

Freedom Within Limits

Respect for oneself, for others, and for the environment — practiced every day.

The Observant, Aware Adult

Trained guides who know when to step in with support and when to let a child explore.

The Prepared Environment

Child-sized furniture, low shelves, and hands-on materials that invite independence.

❧

Kindness, Community & Delight

These three words guide everything we do — from the way we greet each child in the morning to the way we prepare every corner of the classroom.

Frequently Asked Questions

Explore serves children from infancy through age 6. Programs include Explore Piccolo for infants (from 6 weeks), Pre-Primary for toddlers (about 1.5–3 years), and Primary (3–6 years, including kindergarten-aged children).
We have two Bozeman campuses: Explore Academy at 1705 W Kagy Blvd (59715) and Explore Montessori at 1933 N 22nd Ave (59718).
The school day includes drop-off between 7:30–8:30 a.m. and pick-up at 3:30 p.m. Aftercare is available from 3:30–5:30 p.m. with advance arrangement.
Yes. Our tuition schedule includes half-day and school-day options, with different choices by age group. Sibling discounts, scholarships, and a sliding fee scale are available.
We use Brightwheel for daily notes, naps, meals, photos, activities, two-way messaging, and incident reports. Classroom cameras are also available for parent peace of mind.
